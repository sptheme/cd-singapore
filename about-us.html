About Us

EURASIE TRAVEL is one of the oldest and most respected travel agencies in the Kingdom of Cambodia. Established in Lyon (France) in 1988, then in Phnom Penh (Cambodia) in 1990, Eurasie Travel is appreciated for its outstanding services and very competitive prices.

Besides, Eurasie Travel employed 12 staffs with two offices in Phnom Penh and Siem Reap, Land of Angkor Wat, who are strongly motivated and handling efficiently inbound tours to Cambodia, Vietnam and Laos, incentive tours and exhibitions.

We have been in operation successfully with more than twenty partnering agencies worldwide. Our main markets are France, Spain, Germany, Italy, Russia, and Holland.

Due to our most reliable services, efficiencies in all needs of the customers, we become one of the most successful and oldest travel companies in Cambodia.

The Eurasie Travel teams have over 20 years’ experience as inbound tour operators across Cambodia and Indochina, making it one of the oldest tour operators in Cambodia. Our mission has been to promote Cambodia as one of the less visited destinations in the world while offering reliable and professional services.

The philosophy behind our success has been our practice of sharing with local communities. We not only focus on the benefits to us as a privately owned company but also on promoting and involving local businesses and communities so that they too can benefit from Cambodia’s blossoming tourism industry.

EURASIE Travel organizes many services as following:
  • Inbound and outbound tours to Vietnam, Laos, Thailand, Myanmar…
  • Hotel reservation (hotel and home-stay)
  • Boat Ticket
  • Passport and Visa Arrangement
  • Domestic and international air ticketing
  • Transportation in air-conditioned vehicle (sedan, bus, minibus and also 4×4 for the bad road to discover the new destinations
  • M.I.C.E (Meeting, Incentive, Conferences, and Events)
  • Arrangement for groups or individuals, Incentive group, VIP group,
  • Tour guides speaking in French, English, German, Spanish, Chinese, Italian, and Japanese…
Special Tour Program:
